Gluten Free Vegan Chocolate Cake

This chocolate cake will blow you away – with how it tastes and how incredibly easy it is to prepare.

See Recipe

Gluten Free Vegan Chocolate Cake Recipe



Ingredients
  • 2 cups (240 g) gluten free flour
  • 1 1/3 cup (260 g) caster sugar
  • pinch of salt
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 cup (480 mL) non-dairy milk (for instance coconut milk, rice milk or almond milk if not sensitive to nuts)
  • 1 cup (200 g) coconut cream, divided
Instructions
  1. Pre-heat the oven to 355 ºF (180 ºC) and grease two 6 inch (15 cm) round cake tins with vegetable oil. You can also line the bottoms with greaseproof/baking paper.
  2. Mix all dry ingredients together.
  3. Mix the wet ingredients together, and add them to the dry ingredients. Stir well, until you get a smooth cake batter.
  4. Place cherries in a food processor. Pulse just a few times to roughly chop the cherries. Remove half the cherries and place them in a bowl; set aside.
  5. Allow to cool.
  6. In a separate bowl, place the other 1/2 cup of coconut cream. This should be at room temperature or only very slightly cold. Slowly (a tablespoon at a time), stir the chocolate ganache into the coconut cream.
  7. Decorate with raspberries and chocolate shavings, or other decorations of choice.

No Bake Chocolate Bars Recipe



Ingredients
  • 18 Oreo cookies
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon dark chocolate, melted
  • 4 ounces brick-style cream cheese, softened
  • 8 ounces dark chocolate, chopped
  • 3/4 cup heaving whipping cream
  • 1 tablespoon Baileys Irish Cream or Kahlua, optional
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, optional and to taste but recommended
  • 1/2 cup mini semi-sweet chocolate chips
Instructions
  1. Line a 9x9-inch pan with aluminum foil for easier cleanup and spray with cooking spray; set aside.
  2. To the canister of a food processer, add the Oreos (whole) and process on high speed until you have fine crumbs; set aside.
  3. To a small microwave-safe bowl, add the butter, 1 tablespoon chocolate, and heat on high power until the chocolate and butter have melted and can be stirred smooth.
  4. Pour melted chocolate and butter mixture into the food processor, process on high speed to incorporate, turn crumb mixture out into the prepared pan, and hard-pack the mixture to form and even flat crust layer; set aside.
  5. To a large mixing bowl, add the cream cheese, and beat with a handheld electric mixer on high speed until fluffy; set aside.
  6. Transfer the creamy chocolate mixture into the bowl with the cream cheese and beat on high power until smooth and combined.
  7. Evenly spread the mixture over the crust.
  8. Evenly sprinkle with the mini chocolate chips and refrigerate for at least 3 hours, or overnight or a few days in advance, before slicing and serving. After the 3-hour point when dessert is well chilled, cover with foil when dessert is in the fridge.

Moist Chocolate Cupcakes Recipe



Ingredients
  • 1 cup (130g) all purpose flour
  • 1 cup (207g) sugar
  • 6 tbsp (43g) Hershey’s Special Dark Cocoa powder*
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 large egg, room temperaturec
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 cup very hot water
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 300°F (148°C) and prepare a cupcake pan with liners.
  2. Combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl and whisk together.
  3. Add the dry ingredients to a large bowl and whisk together. Set aside.
  4. Combine the egg, buttermilk, vegetable oil and vanilla in another medium sized bowl.
  5. Add the hot water to the batter and mix again, scraping the sides of the bowl as necessary, until combined. Batter will be thin.
  6. Remove the cupcakes from oven and allow to cool for 2 minutes, then remove to a cooling rack to finish cooling.
  7. Cool completely before frosting.

Chocolate Cherry Coconut Butter Cups

They make a tasty snack that’s perfectly bite-sized! Only 5 ingredients, vegan & gluten-free.

See Recipe

Chocolate Cherry Coconut Butter Cups Recipe



Ingredients
  • 10 ounces dark chocolate chips or bars
  • 1/2 cup cherries, pitted
  • 1/4 cup coconut butter
  • 1/2 tablespoon non-dairy milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
Instructions
  1. Line a 12-cup muffin pan with paper liners.
  2. Melt the dark chocolate using a microwave or double burner. If using a microwave, heat in 30 second intervals, stirring between each.
  3. Using half of the melted chocolate, scoop a little chocolate into the bottom of each paper cup. Place in the freezer for 5 minutes to harden.
  4. Place cherries in a food processor. Pulse just a few times to roughly chop the cherries. Remove half the cherries and place them in a bowl; set aside.
  5. Add coconut butter, milk, and vanilla to the food processor with half of the cherries. Blend until smooth.
  6. Scoop about 1/2 tablespoon of the filling mixture into the center of each chocolate cup. Press down gently to flatten the filling.c
  7. Using the remaining chocolate, top each cup with enough chocolate to cover the filling. Place in the freezer for 10 minutes.
